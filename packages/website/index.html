What it does

Route by domain

Send github.com to your work profile and netflix.com to your personal one. Subdomains included.

Route by keyword

Match any URL containing aws, teams, or jira — catches long redirect URLs that domain rules miss.

Works across browsers

Chrome, Brave, Edge, Arc, Helium. Route a link from Chrome to Brave, or any combination. Not just between profiles — between browsers.

Right-click any link

Don't want a permanent rule? Right-click any link and choose "Open in..." to send it to another browser or profile on the spot.

Works across computers

Premium users can route links between their home and work machines. Click a link at home, it opens on your work laptop.

Set it and forget it

Configure your rules once. Every link after that just goes where it should — no clicking, no thinking.
