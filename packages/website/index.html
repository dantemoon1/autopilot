Every link opens in the right browser, automatically.

For people who juggle work, personal, and dev across multiple browsers or profiles. No more copy-pasting URLs between windows.

Get the Extension See Pricing

What it does

Route by domain

Send github.com to work, netflix.com to personal. Subdomains included.

Route by keyword

Catch tricky URLs like aws, teams, or jira redirects that domain rules miss.

Works across browsers

Chrome, Brave, Edge, Arc, Helium. Route links between any combination of browsers and profiles.

Right-click any link

Send any link to another browser on the spot. No permanent rule needed.

Works across computers

Click a link at home, it opens on your work laptop. Premium only. Works anywhere with an internet connection.
