Privacy Policy

autopilot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

The extension reads URLs during navigation solely to determine which browser profile should handle them. This processing happens entirely on your device. No URLs, browsing history, or personal information ever leaves your computer.

URL routing rules are stored locally on your machine in a JSON file. They are never uploaded anywhere.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any third-party services.

If you have questions, open an issue on GitHub.
