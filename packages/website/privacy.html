Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

autopilot ("we", "us") is operated by Dante Moon. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights regarding it.

autopilot operates in two modes. What data we handle depends on which mode you use.

Local mode

In local mode, everything stays on your device. URLs are processed locally to determine routing. Rules are stored in a JSON file on your machine (~/.local/share/autopilot/config.json). No data is transmitted to any server. No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used. We do not collect any personal data in local mode.

Cloud mode

Data we collect and why

Data Purpose Legal basis (GDPR)
Name, email, Google account ID Authentication and account management Contract necessity
Browser profile names Core feature: routing links to the right profile Contract necessity
Routing rules (domains, keywords) Core feature: syncing rules across browsers Contract necessity
Stripe customer ID, subscription status Payment processing and subscription management Contract necessity
URLs (in transit only) Real-time routing between browser instances Contract necessity

URLs transmitted through our relay server for cross-browser routing are never logged, stored, or analyzed. They exist only in memory for the instant needed to deliver them to your other browser.

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate autopilot in cloud mode:

  • Google — authentication via Google OAuth. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing. We never see or store your card details. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Fly.io — server hosting (United States). Subject to Fly.io's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — website hosting. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your data. We do not use analytics or tracking on our website or extension. We do not share your information with any parties beyond those listed above.

International data transfers

Our server is hosted in the United States via Fly.io. If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, your data is transferred to the US for processing. Our service providers (Fly.io, Stripe, Google) are certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, which provides an adequate level of data protection as recognized by the European Commission.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. If you request account deletion, we delete all your data (account, profiles, rules, subscription records) within 30 days. Stripe may retain billing records independently as required by financial regulations.

Cookies and tracking

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. The browser extension uses chrome.storage (local to your browser) to store session data — this is not a cookie and is not accessible to websites.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at dante142@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Children

autopilot is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Automated decision-making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of autopilot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
