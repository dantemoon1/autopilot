The service

autopilot routes links between browser profiles and browsers based on rules you configure. It is available in two modes:

  • Local mode — runs entirely on your device using a native helper. Free, unlimited rules, no account required.
  • Cloud mode — requires a Google sign-in. Rules sync across browsers and devices via our server. Free tier includes 1 routing rule. Premium unlocks additional rules and cross-computer routing.
